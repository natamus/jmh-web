Mob Head Resources

Select the additional mob head texture datapacks you want on the left side, and click on the right arrow to move them to download list. Click the download button to receive the JSON file with the combined data.

Move the downloaded JSON file (starting with jmh-textures) to the location %root%/data/jmh/jmh-textures-date.json.

(Minecraft %root% is where the 'saves'-folder is located)
